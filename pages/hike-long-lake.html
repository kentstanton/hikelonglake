Hike Long Lake, Hike Local

Adirondack hiking is so much more than just bagging peaks. There are lakes and rivers, boreal forests, bogs with insect-eating plants and remote quiet places. So whether you're a Long Lake resident or a visitor, consider nearby trails when planning your next outing. There are plenty of local options to choose from.


New: We have a Youtube channel! The professionally produced videos on HikeLongLake share the incredible beauty of the central Adirondack region. Please take a look, we think you'll enjoy them.


This guide describes hike ranging from well-traveled trails to obscure paths that only locals know about. Everything from hour-long walks to day-long treks into the deep wilderness. And we give you the information you need to find a hike that is right for you. We'll tell you:

The fun: things to see along the way (for adults and kids)

*Directions to the trailhead and where to park

*Difficulty; How long it will take and how many snacks you will need

*Tips for dealing with the bugs and the weather

*Kid and dog friendliness

*Winter; Suitability for Skiing or snowshoeing

For the latest on where to stay and eat, and all the goings on near Long Lake, visit `MyLongLake.com <http://mylonglake.com>`__.
