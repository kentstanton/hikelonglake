Tioga Point on Raquette Lake

In Challenging Hikes

Tioga Point is a New York State Campground located on the northeast shore of Raquette Lake; a fine spot for a rest and a picnic. The trail to Tioga Point covers 6.5 miles, each way, but it is mostly easy walking.**
